Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Dixie Land Tree Service ("we", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

When you request an estimate or contact us, we collect the information you provide — typically your name, phone number, email, address, and a description of the work needed. Our website may also collect anonymous usage data through Google Analytics cookies.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to estimate requests and service inquiries
  • To schedule and perform tree service work
  • To send occasional service updates (only to existing customers)
  • To improve our website and services

Information We Don't Share

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing. We may share information with subcontractors or insurers as necessary to perform the work you hired us for.

Your Choices

You can opt out of any follow-up marketing at any time by emailing info@dixielandtreeservice.com.

Contact

Questions? Email us at info@dixielandtreeservice.com or call (256) 555-0100.
