Huntsville's Tree Care Craftsmen

Dixie Land Tree Service is a locally owned, ISA-certified tree service rooted in North Alabama — built on safe practices, honest pricing, and craftsmanship that shows.

Our Story

Dixie Land Tree Service was founded to bring professional, arborist-led tree care to Huntsville homeowners and businesses who were tired of lowball estimates, surprise damage, and crews that disappear before cleanup. From our first job to the thousands since, our standard has stayed the same: do the work safely, do it right, and leave the property better than we found it.

What a "Certified Arborist" Actually Means

Most companies who call themselves "tree experts" aren't arborists — they're just people with chainsaws. An ISA Certified Arborist has passed a 200-question exam covering tree biology, pruning, cabling, pest identification, soil science, and safe rigging. They maintain the credential by earning 30 continuing-education units every three years.

Every Dixie Land job is supervised by a certified arborist. That means you get real recommendations — not upsells — and work that follows ANSI A300 pruning standards.

Licensed, Insured, Drug-Tested

Credentials You Can Verify

Alabama Tree Surgery (TS) License · General Liability Insurance · Workers' Compensation (Alabama) · ISA Certified Arborist · TCIA best-practice training. Certificates available on request.

Alabama requires tree services to carry a Tree Surgery (TS) Commercial Applicator Permit through the Department of Agriculture, plus a Horticulture Professional Services License. We carry both. We also carry general liability insurance specific to tree work — not generic landscaper coverage — and workers' compensation on every employee.

Built for North Alabama

We grew up around these trees. Huntsville and Madison County have their own particular challenges: fast-moving storm systems from the west, tornado seasons that leave pines down across neighborhoods, and a mix of hardwoods — oak, hickory, pecan, sweetgum — that each demand their own approach. Our crews know the species, the soil, and the weather that shapes them.
