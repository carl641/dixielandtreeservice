Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does tree removal cost in Huntsville?
Tree removal in Huntsville typically ranges from $300 to $2,000+ depending on height, trunk diameter, proximity to structures, and access. We provide free on-site estimates so you know the cost up front.
Do I need a permit to remove a tree?
Most private-property tree removals in Huntsville do not require a permit. Trees in rights-of-way, historic districts, or protected overlays may. We confirm local requirements before every job.
Do you offer 24-hour emergency service?
Yes. We dispatch emergency crews 24/7 for fallen trees, storm damage, and hazards threatening structures. Call (256) 555-0100 any time.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes — we carry full general liability insurance and workers' compensation, and hold the required Alabama Tree Surgery license. Certificates are available on request.
Do you provide free estimates?
Always. On-site estimates are free with no obligation. Most estimates can be scheduled within a day or two.
