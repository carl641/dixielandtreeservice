Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does large tree removal cost in Huntsville?
Large tree removal in Huntsville typically runs $800 to $3,500+. Height, trunk diameter, lean, proximity to the house, and yard access drive the price. Tall pines and mature oaks over 60 feet usually fall in the middle of that range. We provide free on-site estimates.
Can you take down a tree over my house or in a tight backyard?
Yes — that's our specialty. Our remote-controlled tree-removal truck fits through gates and reaches spots a bucket truck can't. We take large trees down piece by piece and keep heavy equipment off your lawn.
Do you offer 24-hour emergency service?
Yes. We dispatch emergency crews 24/7 for fallen trees, storm damage, and large trees threatening your house. Call (256) 497-8735 any time.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes — we carry full general liability insurance and workers' compensation, and hold the required Alabama Tree Surgery license. Certificates are available on request.
Do you provide free estimates?
Always. On-site estimates are free with no obligation, usually within a day or two.
