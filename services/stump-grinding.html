Stump Grinding & Removal in Huntsville, AL

Professional stump grinding that gets rid of eyesores, prevents termites, and opens up your yard for new grass, landscaping, or construction.

Why Grind a Stump?

A leftover stump isn't just ugly — it's a magnet for carpenter ants, termites, and fungi. It also blocks mower paths, sprinkler lines, and any replanting. Grinding removes the problem below grade so you can seed grass, replant, or build.

How Stump Grinding Works

  1. We survey the stump and surrounding area for utilities, sprinklers, and hardscape.
  2. Our grinder (up to 70 hp for large stumps) chips the wood 6–12 inches below grade.
  3. Major surface roots get ground back too — not always possible with DIY rentals.
  4. You choose: leave the grindings to fill the hole, or we haul them off and bring in topsoil.

FAQs

Do you grind the roots?
Surface roots within reach of the grinder, yes. Deep tap roots remain and decay naturally.
Can you fit through my backyard gate?
Our compact grinder fits through 36-inch gates. For tighter spaces we have handheld options.
How soon can I replant or lay sod?
Right away if we haul grindings and topsoil in. If left, let grindings settle and decompose 4–8 weeks.
