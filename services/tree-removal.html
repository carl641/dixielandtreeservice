Tree Removal in Huntsville, AL

Safe, insured tree removal from certified arborists. We handle everything from small backyard oaks to 80-ft pines over homes — with complete cleanup and clear pricing.

When a Tree Needs to Come Down

Not every tree needs removal — and we'll tell you when pruning or cabling is a better option. But when a tree is dead, severely leaning, hollow, infected, or simply in the wrong place for the homeowner's plans, safe removal is the right call. Dixieland's ISA-certified arborists assess every tree before recommending removal, and we document the reasoning in writing.

Trees We Commonly Remove in Huntsville

  • Dying or dead hardwoods — oaks, hickories, and sweetgums that have lost their canopy
  • Storm-damaged trees — split trunks, cracked leaders, uprooted root systems
  • Trees too close to homes — structural risk, foundation roots, or chronic gutter problems
  • Pine decline and borer damage — common across North Alabama after dry seasons
  • Construction-site clearing — new-build lots, additions, driveways, and pools

How Tree Removal Works

  1. Free on-site assessment. A certified arborist inspects the tree, root flare, and surrounding risks.
  2. Written estimate. You get scope, price, and timeline on one page — no verbal surprises.
  3. Site protection. Plywood, rigging, and mats protect what stays.
  4. Controlled dismantling. Climber or bucket truck takes the tree down in sections, or we bring in a crane for heavy loads near structures.
  5. Complete cleanup. Brush chipped, logs cut to length or hauled, yard blown off.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does tree removal cost in Huntsville?
$300–$600 for small trees, $600–$1,200 for medium, $1,200–$2,000 for large, and $2,000+ for crane-required or heavy-access jobs.
Do you remove the stump too?
Stump grinding is separate and can be added at a reduced price when combined with a removal.
Will my insurance cover the removal?
If the tree damaged a covered structure, yes — typically. Preventive removal of a healthy tree is usually not covered.
How quickly can you schedule?
Standard removals are typically scheduled within 3–10 days; emergencies are same-day.
Do you clean up?
Yes. Brush is chipped, logs are hauled (or left if you want firewood), and the yard is blown off before we leave.
