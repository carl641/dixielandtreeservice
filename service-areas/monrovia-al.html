Tree Service in Monrovia, AL

Lot clearing, tree removal, and tree care for the Sparkman-to-James Clemens corridor and Madison County's fastest-growing suburban belt.

Monrovia: New Subdivisions, Mixed Trees

Monrovia has boomed over the last 20 years. The farmland that once sat between Madison and Harvest is now a belt of new neighborhoods served by James Clemens and Sparkman schools. We do a lot of builder lot clearing here, plus newer-home removals of fast-growing species that outgrow their planting spots (Bradford pear, river birch, Leyland cypress).

Common Work

  • Production lot clearing for builder contracts
  • Bradford pear removal — still splitting in spring storms
  • Leyland cypress work — common windbreak that outgrows lots
  • Tree work around new pools and additions
  • Stump grinding in tight suburban lots

Areas Served

Monrovia 35806, Harvest border, Jeff Road corridor, Research Park West, Mount Zion, and all the new subdivisions growing in west Madison County.
