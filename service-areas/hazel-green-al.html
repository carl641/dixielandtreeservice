Tree Service in Hazel Green, AL

Rural-property tree care for north Madison County and the Tennessee border communities.

Hazel Green Tree Work

Hazel Green's agricultural heritage means big open properties with long-standing hardwood fence rows and old homestead trees. We do a lot of aging pecan management here, along with storm response and pasture-edge clearing. North-Alabama squall lines hit Hazel Green hard before rolling into Huntsville — we're often on-site by the time damage is reported in town.

Common Work

  • Pecan limb drop and whole-tree removal
  • Hackberry and osage orange removal along fence rows
  • Large-lot clearing for new home sites and subdivisions
  • Pasture reclaim — cedar and sweetgum encroachment
  • Storm response and roadway clearing

Areas Served

Hazel Green 35750, Meridianville border, Limestone Creek area, Lockhart Road, Butler Mill, and the Tennessee state line.
