Tree Service in Toney, AL

Tree care and land clearing in northwest Madison County — from the Redstone Arsenal border to the Limestone County line.

Toney's Growing, but the Woods Are Still Big

Toney has seen steady new-home growth without losing its rural character. Big lots, mature woodlots, and long driveways are the norm. That means we see a lot of selective clearing — keeping specimen hardwoods while removing pine and underbrush for new homes. Tornado damage is also a recurring issue: March and April squall lines often touch down on this side of Madison County.

Common Work in Toney

  • Selective lot clearing for rural homesites
  • Loblolly and shortleaf pine removal
  • Post oak and hickory pruning
  • Fence-line clearing along large properties
  • Storm damage response

Areas We Serve

Toney 35773, Harvest border, Jeff Road area, Ready Section, Limestone County border, and surrounding rural communities.
