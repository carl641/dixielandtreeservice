Tree Service in Owens Cross Roads, AL

Rural-suburban tree care for south Madison County — Big Cove, Chapman Mountain foothills, and the U.S. 431 corridor.

Big Cove & Beyond

Owens Cross Roads stretches from the base of Chapman Mountain through the cove farmland all the way to the Marshall County line. The mix — mature mountain-foothill hardwoods, working farmland, and a fast-growing residential belt — means we do everything here from specimen oak pruning to lot clearing for new homes.

Species & Common Work

  • Chestnut oak and northern red oak on the mountain foothills
  • Post oak, black oak, and shortleaf pine on the lower slopes
  • Pecan and black walnut around older farmsteads
  • Selective lot clearing for homesites preserving mature trees
  • Storm damage response — the ridge channels wind events

Neighborhoods & Areas

Big Cove Road, Sublett Road corridor, Little Cove, Chapman Mountain foothills, Hampton Cove border, Gurley Pike, Mountain Crest, and 35763.
