Tree Service in Athens, AL

Large tree removal throughout Athens and Limestone County — from mature trees on the Athens State University campus to tall pines on rural farmsteads in north Limestone.

Athens Has Some of North Alabama's Oldest Trees

The historic district around Athens State University holds white oaks, post oaks, and southern magnolias that predate the Civil War. When one of these giants has to come down — from decay, storm damage, or root failure — it takes experience and the right equipment to do it without damaging what's around it.

Outside town, we take down a lot of tall pines and hazard trees for farm homesteads and residential subdivisions across Limestone County.

Athens Services

  • Tree removal
  • Structural pruning of mature historic-district trees
  • Stump grinding
  • 24/7 emergency response

Common Athens Tree Issues

  • Aging white oaks in the historic district
  • Southern magnolia storm damage — heavy wood breaks dramatically
  • Eastern red cedar encroachment in unmanaged pastureland
  • Storm-damaged pecan — especially in older rural homesteads
  • Hackberry decline in urban plantings
