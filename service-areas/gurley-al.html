Tree Service in Gurley, AL

Rural tree work, Paint Rock Valley storm response, and land clearing for eastern Madison County and the Jackson County line.

Gurley's Paint Rock Country

Gurley sits at the edge of Paint Rock Valley — one of the most beautiful and tree-rich corners of North Alabama. Big ridge hardwoods (chestnut oak, hickory, maple) tower over farms and small homesteads. The ridge-valley geography channels storm wind, so we see a lot of wind-thrown tree calls after every major event.

Common Work

  • Ridge-line storm damage response
  • Large specimen oak management
  • Access-challenged rural removals
  • Pasture clearing and cedar reclaim
  • Bottomland hardwood work along the Paint Rock

Areas Served

Gurley 35748, Hurricane Creek, Keel Mountain, Jackson County border, and all of the Paint Rock Valley accessible to Gurley.
